Privacy Policy for Stock Manager

Last updated: November 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Stock Manager ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Business Data: Product information, manufacturing records, sales records, and inventory data you enter into the app
  • Contact Information: Email address (if you contact us for support)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and crash reports

2.3 Third-Party Services

  • Google Drive: If you choose to backup your data, it will be stored on Google Drive (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Google Sign-In: If you sign in with Google, we may access your Google account information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the stock management functionality
  • Process and store your business data locally on your device
  • Enable data backup and restore features (when you opt-in)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • All your business data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • We do not have access to your data unless you explicitly share it

4.2 Cloud Backup (Optional)

  • If you choose to backup data to Google Drive, it will be stored securely in your Google account
  • You can delete backups at any time through the app or Google Drive

4.3 Data Security

  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data
  • Data transmission is encrypted when backing up to Google Drive
  • We recommend using device security features (PIN, biometric authentication)

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • With Your Consent: When you choose to backup data to Google Drive
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party services that help us operate the app (all subject to confidentiality agreements)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View and download your data stored in the app
  • Delete: Delete your data through the app's delete functions
  • Portability: Export your data in standard formats
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop using cloud backup features at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
